The Ensemble · Two models, one prediction

Two estimators arguing until they agree.

XGBoost handles the categorical structure — body style, trim, transmission, seller — the decisions that slice the market into tiers.

A neural network picks up what gradient boosting misses: the continuous drift between mileage and depreciation, the embedding space where model + trim live together.

Both models predict in log-space. Predictions are exponentiated, then combined with weights fit on a validation split. The result is consistently sharper than either model alone.
